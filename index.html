Welcome

        I'm Divyakant Tahlyan, a graduate student at the University of South Florida. Currently, I am pursuing Master of Science in Civil (Transportation) Engineering and working towards my thesis titled 'Route Choice Modeling using Large Streams of GPS Data'. I am also working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering at USF, where my present research revolves around understanding the spatial transferability of travel forecasting models. Before joining USF, I worked at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore as a Junior Research Fellow.
        My research interests are in using statistical and econometric methods in understanding travel behavior with a special interest in discrete choice modeling and travel demand forecasting.
